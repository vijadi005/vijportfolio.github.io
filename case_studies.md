# Case Studies

## Advisor Experience Transformation

Financial advisors operate within complex ecosystems involving multiple platforms, processes and service teams.

This project focused on identifying major friction points and designing a unified experience strategy.

### Key Activities

• Advisor journey mapping  
• Pain point research  
• Opportunity framework development  
• Service blueprint creation  
• Initiative prioritization

### Outcomes

• Identified critical breakdowns in advisor workflows  
• Created experience principles for future platforms  
• Proposed strategic initiatives for platform improvements

---

## Key Themes Identified

Transparency in case status  
Simplification of tools  
Reduced manual follow-ups  
Improved communication flows
