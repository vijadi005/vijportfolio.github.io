Overview

Context

Why this work mattered

Advisors were navigating a fragmented landscape of portals, tools, forms, support channels, and manual processes. This increased effort, introduced avoidable errors, and weakened confidence in Manulife as an easy partner to do business with.

Business need: Improve ease of doing business, strengthen advisor retention, and support growth across insurance and wealth journeys.
User need: Reduce complexity, get faster answers, complete tasks with confidence, and stay informed without constant follow-up.
Design opportunity: Reframe disconnected tools into a coherent end-to-end service experience.
My role

Service design and experience strategy

Mapped advisor journeys, tasks, ecosystems, and supporting backstage processes.
Synthesized pain points, patterns, and opportunity areas across advisor-facing interactions.
Defined future-state concepts, principles, and initiatives for a more seamless digital advisor experience.
Framed the work as a case study narrative suitable for a portfolio and stakeholder storytelling.
02

The challenge

How might we create an integrated advisor journey that reduces friction, improves confidence, and helps advisors bring in, manage, and grow business more effectively?
Business goals

What the organization needed

Increase adoption of digital tools and reduce dependence on manual, paper-based workarounds.
Reduce NIGOs and processing errors through better guidance, validation, and simpler workflows.
Improve advisor satisfaction by creating a more intuitive, transparent, and responsive experience.
Enable growth through better product discovery, smarter guidance, and improved cross-sell opportunities.
Protect relationships so advisors are less likely to shift business to competitors.
Advisor objectives

What advisors were trying to do

Look competent in front of clients and follow through on promises without delays or uncertainty.
Find the right tools quickly without navigating multiple systems and repeated logins.
Submit business correctly the first time and avoid preventable back-and-forth.
Track status clearly for applications, requests, servicing, and in-force changes.
Stay informed and supported through onboarding, training, service, and ongoing business growth.

04

Design principles

Principle 01
Make the journey feel connected
Reduce jumping between tools, repeated sign-ins, and re-entering the same information across systems.
Principle 02
Replace uncertainty with visibility
Use confirmations, trackers, alerts, and next-step guidance so advisors always know what is happening.
Principle 03
Guide, don’t overwhelm
Break complex processes into guided steps with inline help, training, and validation at the moment of need.
Principle 04
Design for confidence
Help advisors feel prepared, informed, and supported so they can serve clients with greater confidence.
Principle 05
Reduce manual effort
Pre-fill data, standardize forms, remove duplicate steps, and simplify backstage dependencies wherever possible.
Principle 06
Create one Manulife experience
Move toward a holistic advisor ecosystem that feels consistent across products, touchpoints, and stages of the journey.
05

Key initiatives

01
Integrated advisor dashboard
A role-based home that brings together status, notifications, opportunities, renewals, claims, support, and business insights in one place.
02
Unified tools and single entry point
Connect illustrations, applications, product information, forms, tracking, and servicing into a coherent workflow instead of isolated destinations.
03
Guided smart forms
Use dynamic validation, contextual prompts, prefilled data, and real-time error prevention to reduce NIGOs and submission friction.
04
Transparent application and request tracking
Introduce a tracker experience with confirmations, milestones, alerts, and next-step guidance for submitted business and in-force changes.
05
Contextual onboarding and learning
Support new and existing advisors with interactive onboarding, just-in-time education, tutorials, CE content, and embedded best practices.
06
Smarter search and discoverability
Improve access to forms, product content, and support through predictive search, filters, AI-guided help, and better information architecture.
07
Proactive communications
Move from reactive service to timely updates, alerts, reminders, and milestone-based communications across the advisor lifecycle.
08
Data-informed opportunity engine
Surface cross-sell opportunities, upcoming maturities, at-risk clients, and relevant recommendations based on advisor and client context.
09
Continuous feedback loop
Create visible ways to capture advisor feedback, prioritize issues, communicate progress, and involve advisors in shaping future improvements.
06

Expected impact

Advisor value

A simpler, more confident way of doing business

Less friction across discovery, application, servicing, and post-sale moments.
Better visibility into what is happening, what is missing, and what comes next.
Faster completion with less manual entry and fewer preventable errors.
Stronger trust in Manulife as a partner that supports advisor success.
Business value

A more scalable service ecosystem

Reduced call volume by improving self-serve clarity and proactive communication.
Lower operational burden through fewer handoffs, fewer NIGOs, and better digital completion.
Higher adoption of tools that better align with real advisor workflows.
Improved retention and growth by making Manulife harder to leave and easier to expand with.
The core shift was not just improving screens. It was redefining the advisor experience from a set of disconnected transactions into a more cohesive service relationship.
