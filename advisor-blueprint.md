# Advisor Digital Experience Blueprint

## Context

Financial advisors rely on multiple disconnected tools to manage client interactions, policy servicing and case tracking.

This project focused on understanding the advisor journey and identifying opportunities to improve efficiency and experience.

---

## The Problem

Advisors face several challenges:

• Lack of visibility into case status  
• Multiple platforms for basic workflows  
• Manual follow-ups with service teams  
• Limited proactive notifications

These issues result in lost productivity and poor advisor experience.

---

## Research & Discovery

To understand the problem space we conducted:

• Advisor workflow analysis  
• Pain point synthesis  
• Journey mapping  
• Service blueprint creation

---

## Key Pain Points

Fragmented tools  
Limited transparency  
Manual servicing processes  
Inconsistent communication

---

## Opportunities

Create unified advisor dashboard  
Provide proactive case updates  
Simplify workflows across platforms  
Improve advisor support visibility

---

## Outcome

The initiative proposed a set of prioritized experience improvements that could significantly reduce friction for advisors while improving overall operational efficiency.
